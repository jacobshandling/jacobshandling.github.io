Bio

Jake Shandling strives to make rhythmically compelling, texturally rich, honest music.

He has worked with artists including Slavic Soul Party!, Peter Stan, Sonny Climes, Zach Moses, Joe Warner, Ben Goldberg, Kellye Gray, and Groundation Next Generation, and studied with Eric McPherson, Eddie Marshall, George Marsh, William Winant, Allison Miller, and Jeff Ballard.
