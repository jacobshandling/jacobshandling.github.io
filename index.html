Jake Shandling is a New York and San Francisco based drummer. He aspires to make rhythmically compelling, texturally rich, honest music.

Jake has worked with artists including Slavic Soul Party!, Adam O'Farrill, Peter Stan, Sonny Climes, Joe Warner, Ben Goldberg, Kellye Gray, and Groundation Next Generation. He has studied with Eric McPherson, Eddie Marshall, George Marsh, William Winant, Allison Miller, and Jeff Ballard.
